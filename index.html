Biography

Jason grew up on Florida’s Gulf Coast and earned a BA in Political Science with a minor in International Relations from Florida State University. After graduation, they worked with the Democratic Party of Florida, organizing town hall events. Their career shifted to NGO work in Nepal and India, where they supported child welfare and taught street children, sparking a passion for education. This led them to Bangkok, Thailand, where they earned a TEFL certificate and taught at various schools. Back in the U.S., they joined Goodwill Industries as a supervisor and instructor, where they also began learning software development skills in JavaScript, HTML, and CSS.
